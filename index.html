Founder Aaron Wexler has had a passion for burgers since his father passed down the original "Burger House" to him in 2007. He has taken the company from a modest, mom and pop diner, to a regional landmark! Join Aaron and the rest of the team at The Burger House and find out what a true passion for beef tastes like!

Our burgers have been rated the best in Kentucky for the past 10 years! The Burger Trap has also been featured on Food Network!
